About Our Heroes

The story of these two heroes stretches back a while. While they both love each other dearly, Martin was the one that takes the risks in the name of love. She is certainly in this relationship as much as Martin, but she is more of a rough, rugged kinda girl, not wanting to show feelings unless private. Either way, both love each other dearly, and hope all guests enjoy the wedding as much as they will!
