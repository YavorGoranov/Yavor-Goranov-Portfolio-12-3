Travel to Martin Land

Where is it located?

The island is located on a very remote distance in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. A mentally disabled millioner named Martin Palikarsky bought the island a long time ago and has populatted it with a few things.

What can you see in Martin Land?

When you visit Martin Land there ar many beautiful and interesting place. The first place I recommend you to visit is the house where Martin Palikarsky lived. It is a hounded house. There is a story, which says that after his dead, a witch casted a spell on him, making his body become one with the house.

While visiting the house I reccomend you taking a tour of it. Inside you will be able to see how the crazy Martin leaved. You can see things like:

  • the room where Martin tortured innocent people
  • his strange orianted living room
  • the bedroom with a huge artillery of guns

Another thing, which I recommend when visiting is having breakfast in Molly's cafes. It is one of the only "restaraunts" on the island, but it serves the BEST waffles in the world. You can pick whatever toping you want and enjoy it with a side of a milkshake. In the cafe you can also read or ask about the story of how Molly's place was created - it is related to Martin The Crazy.

What to bring?

  1. Suncream
  2. Warm clothes
  3. A lot of smiles

I have been to Martin Land a lot of times and I really recommend it to anybody who likes horror movies or would like to feel a bit of adrenaline go up his veins!
